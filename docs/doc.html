Rules of searching:

To start your search you must fill out the form:

a) to find books by author - input his lastname;
b) to find books by publisher - input name of publisher;
c) to find books by year of bublisher - input a number;
d) to find books by keywords - input one keyword or more keywords separated by a white space;
e) if you fill many fields, searching will be by only first field!
